### Responsibilities

Maintainers agree to:

- Review and respond to pull requests within 5 business days
- Triage and respond to new issues within 3 business days
- Help guide the technical direction of the project
- Enforce the [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)
- Ensure contributions meet the standards outlined in [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md)

### Becoming a Maintainer

Contributors who consistently demonstrate:

- High-quality code contributions
- Thoughtful code reviews
- Active participation in issue discussions

may be invited to become maintainers. Maintainership is granted by consensus of existing core maintainers.
